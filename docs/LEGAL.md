# Legal and Content Boundaries

This document is project guidance, not legal advice.

## Rules content

The project may implement and reproduce content made available in the Systems
Reference Document 5.2.1 under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International,
subject to the exact attribution and license requirements accompanying that
document.

Before public distribution:

1. preserve the required SRD attribution text exactly;
2. include the CC BY 4.0 notice and license link;
3. audit every class, subclass, species, feat, spell, monster and rule text;
4. remove protected settings, characters, logos, trade dress and product text
   that are not part of the licensed material;
5. obtain a qualified legal review.

## Original identity

The following must be original unless a source is separately licensed:

- game title treatment and logo;
- world names, history and cosmology;
- cultures, factions and divinities;
- narrative prose and dialogue;
- visual art, UI, music and sound;
- additional species, subclasses, monsters, spells and items.

## Branding

Do not market the project as an official Dungeons & Dragons product. The working
repository name may change before public branding review. Do not use official
logos or imply endorsement.

## Asset provenance

Every external asset requires a record containing author, source URL, license,
modifications and attribution requirements. Assets without provenance cannot
enter a release build.
