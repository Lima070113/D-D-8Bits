# Art Direction

## Visual promise

The game uses modern detailed pixel art with the readability of a tactical board
and the atmosphere of a living fantasy world. It should feel handcrafted even
when the world is procedurally assembled.

## Core language

- isometric silhouettes readable at normal gameplay zoom;
- cool navy and teal environments contrasted by warm fire and magic;
- restrained saturation outside focal points;
- chunky pixel clusters rather than blurred or painterly surfaces;
- terrain communicates rules before overlays are needed;
- heroic wonder can coexist with dark ruins and visible consequences.

## Tactical readability

- blue identifies the player hero;
- restrained crimson identifies hostile creatures;
- amber identifies fire, actions and important interactable elements;
- cyan identifies water and cold surfaces;
- elevation uses visible side faces and value separation;
- cover uses physical props plus a UI explanation when selected.
